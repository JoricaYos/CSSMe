My Life Story


Being the eldest daughter in our family, I've shouldered both the joys and challenges that come with responsibility from a young age.


Growing up without a father, his presence was deeply missed, but his memory serves as a guiding light in my journey. While he may be in God's hands, his values of strength, resilience, and love continue to shape who I am today.


Navigating through life's twists and turns has taught me invaluable lessons in perseverance, empathy, and the power of familial bonds. As a BSIT student, I bring this unique perspective to my studies, infusing my work with compassion and determination. Join me as I blend my technical expertise with the warmth and understanding cultivated from my life experiences, striving to make a positive impact in both the digital realm and the world around me
